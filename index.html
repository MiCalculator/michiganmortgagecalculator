Glossary & Important Notes

Taxable Value (Assumed)
Assumes taxable value equals 50% of home price. Actual taxable value is set by the local assessor.
Uncapping
After a sale, taxable value may increase due to uncapping.
Millage Rates
Millage rates are set by local units and are subject to change.
Special Assessments
Special assessments may apply and are not always included.
Property-Specific Factors
Exemptions, abatements, uncapping, and special assessments may affect actual taxes. Contact us for a more detailed, property-specific analysis.
