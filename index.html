Glossary & Important Notes

Taxable Value (Assumed)
This calculator assumes taxable value equals 50% of the home price. Actual taxable value is determined by the local assessor.
Uncapping
After a transfer of ownership, Michigan law may remove the prior taxable value cap, potentially increasing property taxes.
Millage Rates
Millage rates are set by local governments and school districts and are subject to change.
Special Assessments
Sewer, water, road, lake, or other special assessments may apply and are not always included in millage rates.
Property-Specific Factors
Exemptions, abatements, uncapping, special assessments, and unique circumstances can affect actual taxes. Contact us for a more detailed, property-specific analysis.
