Privacy Policy

Last updated:

What we collect

  • Calculator inputs: values you type into the calculator (home price, down payment, etc.) are used to generate estimates in your browser.
  • Analytics: we use Google Analytics to understand site traffic (for example: pages viewed and general location/ device data). This helps improve the site.
  • Lead form information: if you request to be connected with a lender or realtor, you may provide contact info (name, phone, email, etc.).

How lead forms work

The “Connect with a lender” and “Connect with a realtor” forms open your email app using a mailto link. That means the information is sent using your email client and provider.

How we use information

  • To provide mortgage and tax estimates.
  • To respond to requests and connect you with a lender or realtor (if you submit a connection request).
  • To improve the site through analytics.

Sharing

If you request to be connected with a lender or realtor, we may share your submitted information with a partner lender or real estate professional for the purpose of contacting you and providing services.

Cookies

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.
